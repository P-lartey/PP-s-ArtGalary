My Artistic Journey

From Childhood Passion to Artistic Expression

Since I was a little boy, I have had the desire and natural talent for drawing and painting using indigenous tools like charcoal, pencil, and ink. As I grew up, my skill and desire for art improved, and I could depict reality using basic art tools and materials.

In high school, I poured my heart into creating artwork that showcased my curiosity and love for exploration. Although Arts may not be my main focus or ultimate goal, these pieces hold a special place in my heart. Each artwork represents my ability to bring imagination to life, embrace risks, and transform ideas into tangible creations. More importantly, they symbolize my growth and passion for Arts.

My work primarily focuses on capturing everyday Ghanaian life, from bustling market scenes to quiet moments of human connection, using a variety of mediums including mosaic, paint, and charcoal.
